    Famous Acting Duos Who Always Appear In Movies Together

    We often see many pairs of famous actors work together in different films, and sometimes, casting directors will even hire them together to ensure on-screen chemistry on the new project. In most cases, it's a testament to the stars' genuine friendship and professionalism on and off the screen.

    14 Aug 2024, 00:00

    We often see many pairs of famous actors work together in different films, and sometimes, casting directors will even hire them together to ensure on-screen chemistry on the new project. In most cases, it's a testament to the stars' genuine friendship and professionalism on and off the screen.

    "The older you get, the more you treasure that idea of someone knowing your family and where you came from, and being around during these times and these times. Tina and I, we don't have any sisters. So we've rented them," Amy Poehler, who'd become TV's most beloved sisters with Tina Fey, told Glamour in 2015 about their friendship and professional relationship.

    Related: How James Franco Ruined A Quiet Neighbourhood With His Move

    Other than Amy Poehler and Tina Fey, there are some other really genuine Hollywood friendships: Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson and Kevin Hart, Leonardo DiCaprio and Jonah Hill, James Franco and Seth Rogen, and more. They say friends who act in films together, stay together - so to sum it up, here are pairs of actors who always collaborate.

    8 James Franco - Seth Rogen

    James Franco and Seth Rogen are a godly pair of a comedic duo. They'd shared the stage since the 1990s in Freaks & Geeks, The 40-Year-Old Virgin, Pineapple Express, Funny People, The Interview, and more. However, their relationship was strained following the long-lasting sexual assault allegations against Franco, and it seems like the bromance won't recover anytime soon.

    "We aren’t working together right now, and we don’t have any plans to work together. Of course, it was hurtful in context, but I get it, you know, he had to answer for me because I was silent," Franco broke his silence in 2021.

    7 Kristen Stewart - Jesse Eisenberg

    Kristen Stewart and Jesse Eisenberg are not your regular acting soulmates. Their chemistry has been proven from time to time, with big box office hits like the likes of Adventureland in 2009, American Ultra in 2015, and Cafe Society in 2016.

    "I'm not embarrassed around him, ever. He's a really, really, open, warm person. He's a good actor," Steward said of her co-star, adding, "I've known him for years, so to play someone so outside of myself was easy with him because I could do anything and not ever feel silly."

    6 Matt Damon - Ben Affleck

    Ben Affleck and Matt Damon go all the way back to the 1980s when they were kids after their mothers introduced them to each other. They acted together as extras in 1989's Field of Dreams and would attend auditions together in the early 1990s. Now, decades later, they will work together once again in an upcoming film on Nike's quest to sign Michael Jordan. Affleck will play the company's co-founder Phil Knight while Damon will star as Sonny Vaccaro.

    Related: Are James Franco And Seth Rogen Still Friends?

    5 Chris Evans - Scarlett Johansson

    Before they were superheroes, Chris Evans and Scarlett Johansson kicked off their acting career as teen heartthrobs. They go all the way back to 2004, when they starred in a teen comedy-heist film called The Perfect Score. At the time, Johansson, who was 20, had already been quite famous while Evans was just climbing her way up to acting respect. Fast-forward to the late 2010s, they are responsible for some of the biggest, highest-grossing films of all time.

    4 Ben Stiller - Owen Wilson

    While Hollywood can be a hostile place for friendships, Ben Stiller and Owen Wilson's bond is just too strong to be separated. The lifelong friends starred for the first time together in Jim Carrey's The Cable Guy in 1996, followed by 1998's Permanent Midnight. They put their on-screen chemistry to test once again in Zoolander, Meet the Parents, and the Night at the Museum franchise.

    Related: Everything We Know About James Franco And Seth Rogen's Relationship

    3 Jonah Hill - Leonardo DiCaprio

    Leonardo DiCaprio and Jonah Hill are two acting geniuses in their own respective well-known genres: drama and comedy. However, when they linked up for Django Unchained in 2012, many fans wondered how their relationship will translate into the film. It wasn't until 2013's The Wolf of Wall Street that fans got their first-ever taste of DiCaprio and Hill's on and off-screen chemistry. Their last project together, Don't Look Up, was released in 2021.

    2 Leonardo DiCaprio - Kate Winslet

    1997's Titanic sees DiCaprio, who was 22 at the time, partnering up with Kate Winslet as two ill-fated lovers during the chaotic night of the Titanic sinking. They co-starred again in the domestic drama Revolutionary Road, and they've been best friends since then.

    "I couldn’t stop crying," she told The Guardian ahead of their reunion in 2021. "I’ve known him for half my life! It’s not as if I’ve found myself in New York or he’s been in London and there’s been a chance to have dinner or grab a coffee and a catchup. We haven’t been able to leave our countries."

    1 Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson - Kevin Hart

    Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson and Kevin Hart have the best comedic timing in Hollywood, hence their friendship is so pure and professional at the same time. They've co-starred in plenty of films, most notably in the Jumanji series and Central Intelligence. They would go on to troll each other on social media for years, and they're currently gearing up for the second sequel of the modern-day Jumanji film.
