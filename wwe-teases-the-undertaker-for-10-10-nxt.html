    29 Aug 2024, 00:00

    WWE teased a potentially huge guest appearance for next week’s episode of NXT.

    During tonight’s episode of WWE SmackDown, an episode for the October 10 episode of NXT aired. At the end of the commercial, though, a very familiar noise played in the form of Undertaker’s iconic bell ringing.

    Fans quickly flocked to social media to discuss the idea, speculating on what just might happen next Tuesday. The news comes following reports that the WWE legend will be at the WWE Performance Center next week, just in time for a ton of high-profile WWE talent to appear on NXT.
