    What is Thin Gloss and Who is the Founder of Thin Gloss?

    Thin Gloss is a weight loss lip gloss that offers a unique approach to appetite control. Designed specifically for women, this innovative product aims to help individuals achieve their weight loss goals by reducing cravings and suppressing hunger.

    The founder of Thin Gloss is April Morris, a passionate entrepreneur and single mother of two. April’s own struggles with weight loss led her on a journey of extensive research and experimentation. Inspired by the South African plant called Hoodia, known for its appetite control properties, April combined this powerful ingredient with scents that improve mood and vitality to create Thin Gloss.

    With Thin Gloss, April aimed to empower women to take control of their health and well-being. By harnessing the natural benefits of Hoodia and combining them with the convenience and versatility of a lip gloss, she developed a product that addresses both physical and emotional aspects of weight loss.

    April’s belief in the potential of Thin Gloss and her determination to help others led her to introduce this unique product to the market.

    The Journey of Thin Gloss on Shark Tank

    In Season 2 Episode 5 of Shark Tank, April Morris, the founder of Thin Gloss, presented her innovative weight loss lip gloss to the panel of Sharks. Her goal was to secure an investment of $80,000 in exchange for a 5% stake in her company, valuing it at $400,000.

    During her pitch, Morris shared her personal struggles with weight loss and explained how Thin Gloss offered a unique approach to appetite control. Combining the appetite-suppressing properties of Hoodia, a South African plant, with mood-enhancing scents, Thin Gloss aimed to help women achieve their weight loss goals.

    Despite Morris’s compelling story and the potential of the product, the Sharks had concerns. They questioned the lack of clinical testing, legal considerations, and the company’s ability to scale from modest sales figures to a million-dollar business.

    As a result, none of the Sharks chose to invest in Thin Gloss, leading to its journey on Shark Tank coming to an end.

    Although Thin Gloss did not achieve the desired outcome on Shark Tank, its journey highlights the difficulties faced by entrepreneurs in the highly competitive beauty industry. It serves as a reminder that even innovative products may face obstacles on their path to success.

    Thin Gloss Competitors

    Thin Gloss faced strong competition from two notable brands in the lip gloss and beauty industry: Too Faced and Mojo Spa. Both brands offered similar products that targeted the same market as Thin Gloss. Let’s take a closer look at these competitors:

    Too Faced

    Too Faced is a well-established cosmetics brand known for its innovative and high-quality beauty products. They offer a diverse range of lip glosses that not only enhance the lips’ appearance but also provide various benefits. With their extensive product line and loyal customer base, Too Faced posed a significant challenge to Thin Gloss in the market.

    Mojo Spa

    Mojo Spa is another notable competitor in the lip gloss and beauty industry. They are known for their unique and handmade products that are crafted with natural ingredients. Mojo Spa’s lip glosses are highly regarded for their moisturizing properties and long-lasting effects. With their focus on natural ingredients and commitment to sustainability, Mojo Spa appealed to a niche market that directly competed with Thin Gloss.

    These competitors posed challenges to Thin Gloss in terms of product quality, brand reputation, and customer loyalty. The competition was fierce, with each brand vying for a share of the lip gloss market. However, Thin Gloss aimed to differentiate itself through its weight loss and appetite control properties, offering a unique selling proposition that set it apart from its competitors.

    Updates on Thin Gloss after Shark Tank

    After its appearance on Shark Tank, Thin Gloss experienced a temporary boost in sales and website traffic. The exposure from the show generated significant interest in the weight loss lip gloss, leading to an initial surge in consumer demand. However, despite the initial sales success, Thin Gloss faced challenges in sustaining the momentum.

    As time went on, interest in the product declined, and sales gradually decreased. The company struggled to compete with other established brands in the beauty industry, which offered similar products and had a larger market presence. Additionally, the lack of ongoing investment and resources hindered Thin Gloss’s ability to adapt to changing consumer preferences and marketing strategies.

    Ultimately, these difficulties led to the discontinuation of manufacturing and sales in 2012. Thin Gloss is currently out of business, and its products are no longer available for purchase.

    The Story behind Thin Gloss

    The creation of Thin Gloss is rooted in April Morris’ personal weight loss journey and her determination to help others achieve their goals. Inspired by her own struggles with weight loss, April embarked on a mission to develop a product that would not only enhance physical appearance but also provide tangible results.

    As April applied regular lip gloss one day, she had a thought: Could there be a way to incorporate a scent into lip gloss that could aid in weight loss? This question sparked her curiosity and set her on a path of extensive research.

    During her research, April discovered Hoodia, an herb known for its appetite control properties. Seeing the potential in combining Hoodia with the sensory experience of scents, she envisioned a lip gloss that would not only enhance beauty but also contribute to weight loss efforts.

    Determined to bring her vision to life, April invested all her savings into the development of Thin Gloss. With unwavering belief in the product’s potential, she poured her heart and soul into creating a lip gloss that would help women lose weight and improve their self-esteem.

    Combining her entrepreneurial spirit, scientific knowledge, and personal experience, April successfully formulated Thin Gloss, a unique product designed to support weight loss journeys. The lip gloss combines the appetite-suppressing effects of Hoodia with uplifting scents that improve mood and vitality.

    Through her dedication and unwavering commitment, April Morris transformed a simple idea into a tangible solution that empowers women to achieve their weight loss goals. The creation of Thin Gloss stands as a testament to April’s resilience and belief in the potential of her product.

    The Outcome of the Thin Gloss Shark Tank Pitch

    Despite not receiving a deal from the Sharks, Thin Gloss experienced a surge in sales and achieved its million-dollar goal after its appearance on Shark Tank. The weight loss claims made during the pitch, however, raised concerns about FDA regulations and the lack of clinical testing.

    During the pitch, founder April Morris highlighted how Thin Gloss could aid in weight loss through its unique formulation. Although the Sharks expressed interest, they ultimately decided not to invest due to concerns about the product’s weight loss claims and the lack of scientific evidence to support its effectiveness.

    While Thin Gloss saw an increase in sales and website traffic following its appearance on the show, the company is no longer available for sampling. The FDA’s concerns and the absence of clinical testing likely played a role in Thin Gloss’s inability to secure a deal with the Sharks. Although the product achieved its financial goal, these concerns ultimately impacted its long-term viability.
