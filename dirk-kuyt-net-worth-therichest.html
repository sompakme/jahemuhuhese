    Dirk Kuyt is a professional football player who currently plays for the Fenerbahçe as the team's forward. Prior to Fenerbahçe, he played for Utrecht, Feyenoord, and Liverpool.

    Since 2004, Kuyt has been a part of the Netherlands national football team which participated in four major international football tournaments such as the 2010 and 2006 World Cup, as well as the Euro 2008 and 2012.

    Dirk Kuyt was born on July 22, 1980 in Katwijk aan Zee, Netherlands. His professional football career began with Utrecht, which he played for from 1998 through 2003. From 2003 through 2006, he played for Feyenoord. He then spent the next six years with Liverpool, and since 2012, he has played for the Fenerbahçe.

    He is currently married to Gertrude Kuyt. He has four children named Roan, Aidan, Noelle, and Jordn Kuyt.
