    American mystery thriller series Outer Range is currently streaming on Amazon Prime Video. The first season of the series which was created by Brian Watkins has eight episodes and two episodes are released each week starting on its scheduled premiere on April 15, 2022. The series is about the Wyoming rancher Royal Abbott played by Josh Brolin is became somewhat involved in a mystery surrounding some supernatural activity. The family is coping with the disappearance of Perry Abbott’s wife disappearance while the Tillersons made a play for their own ranch. The supernatural mystery happened as soon as the drifter with a connection to Abbott's ranch, Autumn, has arrived in Wyoming. The series has a jam-packed cast members and listed below are the cast members of the series.

    Imogen Poots

    Imogen Poots plays in the series as the itinerant woman named Autumn who has convinced the Abbotts using her charms to let her camp on their land. She has a sketchy past and is seeking some cosmic truths. English actress Imogen Poots currently has a net worth of $2 million. She was born in Hammersmith, London on June 1989. Imogen is best known for her portrayal in the film 28 Weeks Later.

    Shaun Sipos

    Shaun Sipos plays in the series as the arch-rival of the Abbott family, the vengeful and toxic Luke Tillerson. He is the one running the day-to-day activities of their family ranch. He runs the operations of their ranch with a ruthless competitive streak who does not want to lose to anyone. Canadian actor Shaun Sipos currently has a net worth of $2 million. He was born on October 1981 in Victoria, British Columbia. He is best known for his portrayal as Nick Gibson in the TV series called Maybe It's Me. He made his first ever film debut starring in the film Final Destination 2 in 2003.

    Tamara Podemski

    Tamara Podemski plays as Deputy Sheriff Joy in the series. She is a lifelong cop who is running for the county sheriff. She is the first ever gay Native American to do run for the position in Wyoming. Canadian actress Tamara Podemski currently has a net worth of $3 million. She was born on October 16, 1977 in Toronto, Canada. She mainly accumulated her wealth from her primary profession which is acting.

    Noah Reid

    Noah Reid plays in series as youngest of the three Tillerson brothers, Billy Tillerson. He is very different from his siblings as he ethereal and unsettling. He often sings just to pour his heart out. Canadian-American actor Noah Reid currently has an estimated net worth of $ 4 million. He is best known for his portrayal of the character Patrick Brewer on Schitt's Creek. He has starred on the critically-acclaimed Canadian TV series from 2017 until 2020. Aside from the Canadian TV series, he has starred on numerous projects such as Score: A Hockey Musical in 2010, Kevin from Work in 2015 and The Archivists in 2020.

    Lili Taylor

    Lili Taylor plays in the series as the loyal matriarch of the Abbott family. She has a warm and salt-of-the-earth personality who has a deep faith which was tested through the episodes. American actress Lili Taylor currently has a net worth of $4 million. She was born on February 20, 1967 in Glencoe, Illinois.     Lewis Pullman plays in the series as the youngest in the family, Rhett Abbott. He is most rebellious son of Royal and Cecilia who wants to become the best bull rider. American actor Lewis Pullman’s estimated current net worth is $18 million. He was born on January 29, 1993 in Los Angeles, California.     Tom Pelphrey

    Tom Pelphrey plays in the series as eldest and most dutiful in the Abbott family, Perry Abbott. He is bothered about the mystery of how his wife Rebecca went missing. Perry is an expert rancher who is always doing his best to provide for his daughter Amy as a single dad. American actor Tom Pelphrey was born in Howell, New Jersey on July 28, 1982. Although it is a bit odd, he celebrates his birthday every year on July 28th.

    Josh Brolin

    Josh Brolin plays as Royal Abbott who is born and bred in Wyoming and can be described as the last real cowboy. He is an unsettled settler and believes that home is everything.
