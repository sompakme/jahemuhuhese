    Finding the Best CBD Gummies

    There are tons of CBD gummies on the market, and navigating the endless options is overwhelming. To help you get through some of the products, we’ve compiled a list of our top CBD gummies for a variety of potential benefits.

    Our guide is an excellent place to start and will hopefully help you narrow down a few options to find the right CBD gummies for your needs.

    How We Chose Our Top 12 CBD Gummies

    There’s certainly no shortage of CBD gummies for sale out there. To separate the winners from a sea of competitors, we narrowed the field by focusing on these quality indicators:

    • Clean CBD sourcing
    • Third-party lab tests made available to the public
    • Companies that are mission-driven and give back
    • Efficiency (effectiveness of the product)

    Clean CBD Sourcing

    It’s important to know where your CBD comes from and how it was produced. We took the guesswork out of this for you by finding out how companies are sourcing and processing their CBD gummies. All the CBD products on this list are made with CBD hemp grown and extracted in the USA. There is no imported CBD oil or untraceable vendor in the top 12.

    Available Third-Party Lab Tests

    Not only did we make sure all the CBD gummies we selected have current, third-party lab results available, we actually looked at the results! These verify that the CBD products on our list are safe for consumption, free of contaminants, and match the amount of CBD advertised.

    Mission-Driven Brands

    Because there really are so many CBD gummies out there, narrowing our focus to mission-driven brands means these are purchases our readers can feel good about. The charitable CBD companies on this list are involved in all kinds of ways of giving back through compassionate discounts, volunteering, donating to nonprofits, and more.

    Efficiency

    Finally—and arguably, most importantly—we had to make sure there’s some sort of evidence that the CBD gummies in question actually work. We analyzed test results for potency to measure this, tried some of the CBD products ourselves, and rummaged through hundreds of verified reviews online to see what people really have to say.
