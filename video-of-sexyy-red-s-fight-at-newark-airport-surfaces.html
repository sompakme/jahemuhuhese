    Sexyy Red was involved in a brawl at Newark Liberty International on Saturday (June 8) and now a video has surfaced showing the entire ordeal.

    TMZ shared the clip on Tuesday (June 11), showing the “Skee Yee” rapper and her entourage getting into it with another group, which resulted in multiple arrests. In the video, the St. Louis star can be seen walking up with a stanchion as if she was preparing to hit someone with it.

    Fortunately, a guy can be seen pulling her away, keeping her from getting into the thick of it. However, it appears that Red, né Janae Nierah Wherry, stepped out of frame and continued to possibly get physical.

    See below.

    On Tuesday (June 11), the New Jersey Port Authority issued a statement to XXL.

    “At 1:20 a.m. on Saturday, June 8th, PAPD were called to respond to a fight in progress at EWR Terminal C, gate 82,” the statement reads. “Two victims were found to have lacerations to their heads. The two males accompanying Ms. Wherry, Leslie Baptiste and Carl Thompson, were arrested for assault, and Ms. Wherry was arrested for disorderly conduct. The male victims were transported to University Hospital with minor injuries.”

    Reportedly, on Monday morning (June 10) Sexyy revealed that she was released from detainment with a message on Instagram. She also shared that she was hanging with Kodak Black. “I just got out, good morning,” she said in a clip.

    She also offered to replace a phone that was damaged during the brawl. “To the people that was in that altercation at the airport please dm me so I can replace yo phone !!”
