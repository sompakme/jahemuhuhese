    What is Sanaia Applesauce? Who is the founder of Sanaia Applesauce?

    Sanaia Applesauce is a premium apple sauce brand founded by Keisha Smith-Jeremie. The brand takes inspiration from the fruits of the Old World and offers a unique combination of tartness, sweetness, and tanginess. Keisha, the founder, built the brand upon her success in human resources and her childhood memories in the Bahamas.

    Sanaia Applesauce caters to adult taste buds and offers flavors like guava and lavender pear. The brand aims to provide a healthy snack option that indulges the senses with gourmet flavors. The fruit-forward combinations and high-quality ingredients make Sanaia Applesauce stand out in the competitive market.

    Keisha Smith-Jeremie’s passion for creating a premium apple sauce brand led to the establishment of Sanaia Applesauce. With her background in HR and her desire to share the flavors of her childhood, Keisha has crafted a product that brings a taste of the Bahamas to consumers’ homes.

    Through Sanaia Applesauce, Keisha aims to offer a unique and enjoyable eating experience that surpasses traditional apple sauce options. The brand’s commitment to superior quality and innovative flavors has garnered attention and recognition among health-conscious consumers.

    As the founder of Sanaia Applesauce, Keisha Smith-Jeremie has led the brand’s growth and garnered a loyal following. With her vision and determination, Sanaia Applesauce continues to delight customers with its premium apple sauce offerings.

    Sanaia Applesauce Shark Tank Pitch

    During the Shark Tank pitch, Keisha Smith-Jeremie, the founder of Sanaia Applesauce, showcased her inspiring story and presented a compelling investment opportunity. Offering a 15% equity stake in exchange for a $150,000 investment, Keisha emphasized the potential of Sanaia Applesauce as a unique and gourmet brand in the market.

    Keisha’s pitch highlighted the extensive research and successful proof-of-concept test that Sanaia Applesauce underwent, generating an impressive $40,000 in revenue. This demonstrated the brand’s viability and market demand, garnering attention from the Sharks.

    “Sanaia Applesauce presents an innovative and delicious twist on a beloved childhood snack. With our organic ingredients and unique flavors, we aim to cater to the more sophisticated palates of adults,”

    During the pitch, Mark Cuban, renowned investor and Shark Tank panelist, was captivated by the potential of Sanaia Applesauce. He made an offer of $150,000 for a 25% stake in the company. However, due to a premature celebration during the pitch, the deal was not finalized.

    Keisha’s compelling pitch on Shark Tank provided a platform to showcase Sanaia Applesauce’s premium quality and innovative flavors, leaving a lasting impression on both the Sharks and viewers alike.

    Sanaia Applesauce Shark Tank Updates

    Since its appearance on Shark Tank, Sanaia Applesauce has faced its fair share of challenges and setbacks. The COVID-19 pandemic caused disruptions in production, forcing the company to temporarily halt operations. However, Sanaia Applesauce recently made an exciting announcement in April 2022 – a relaunch.

    The company has made its unsweetened and guava flavors available for purchase on its website and also on Amazon. These unique and delicious flavors are sure to please consumers looking for a healthy and indulgent treat. But the relaunch doesn’t stop there.

    Sanaia Applesauce has plans to expand its flavor offerings even further. In addition to the existing flavors, the company is set to launch blackberry and hibiscus flavors, catering to a wider range of taste preferences. This expansion showcases the brand’s commitment to innovation and providing delightful options for its customers.

    Despite the challenges faced, Sanaia Applesauce has shown resilience and continues to make strides in the market. As of November 2021, the company has generated an impressive annual revenue of $4.5 million, a testament to its growing popularity and success.
