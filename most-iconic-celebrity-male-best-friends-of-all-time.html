    Whether in friendships or romance, it is not uncommon to see celebrities together for what feels like only a minute. But amid the short-lived love affairs and platonic relationships, some stars have proved that not even the warped Hollywood status can stop them fr0m building long-lasting bonds. Of course, this has been inspiring to see and they have definitely warmed all of our hearts over the years.

    Related: The Most Iconic Celebrity Female Best Friends Of All Time

    But while female friendships have become notoriously popular, Hollywood men are showing that loving each other and being super adorable is not restricted to women alone. From actors to television personalities to music icons and many more, here are some of the best bromances the showbiz business has ever seen!

    9 Andy Cohen And Anderson Cooper

    You’d think the similarities between Andy Cohen and Anderson Cooper don’t go beyond both men being on your TV screens and sharing identical initials, right? Wrong! they are actually what many would call two peas in a pod. First set up on a blind date in the early '90s, Cohen and Cooper have nurtured a friendship spanning over two decades. Even more impressive about these two best friends is how they have been by each other's sides over the years. The pair's incredible bond has also been recently strengthened by their roles as new fathers. Great friends, great co-workers, and doting fathers are just a few of the highlights of these TV gods' friendship. Such BFF goals!

    8 Leonardo DiCaprio And Tobey McGuire

    Sharing the big screen as buddies in the 2013 movie, The Great Gabsy does not even begin to scratch the surface of the-30-year long friendship shared between Leonardo DiCaprio and Tobey Maguire. The pair first met as young actors at an audition in the late '80s and have since remained friends. From working together to partying together and becoming neighbors at some point, there’s no breaking the special bond between DiCaprio and Maguire. Not even the warped Hollywood fame can try. In a world where Celebrity friendships crash and burn even before taking off, Leonardo and Tobey’s friendship is sure a model for the upcoming ones.

    7 Adam Levine And Blake Shelton

    It is quite unclear when singers Adam Levine and Blake Shelton first met but the duo's adorable friendship went public during their time as judges on NBC's The Voice. Though Adam left the show after season 16, that has not gotten in the way of his bromance with Blake. Most significant about their friendship is the speculation that it was Adam who introduced Blake to his now-wife Gwen Stefani who also serves as a judge on The Voice.

    6 Chris Hemsworth And Tom Hiddleston

    Chris Hemsworth and Tom Hiddleston may play your favorite MCU characters but these two are definitely more than just co-actors. Since first meeting on the set of Thor in 2009, Chris and Tom have become the best of friends, giving Hollywood one of its finest bromances. When they are not acting together, Chris and Tom never fail to support and celebrate each other's achievements and that is something we definitely do love to see! They also share enthusiasm for the same kind of movies and are also super supportive of each other’s achievements. We love to see it!

    5 Ben Affleck And Matt Damon

    Ben Affleck reportedly met Matt Damon in the early ’80s when they were just 8 and 10 years old respectively, meaning they have been friends for decades. In their years as best friends, these two have risen to the top of their careers with Affleck widely known for his role as Batman and Damon popular for portraying Jason Bourne in the Bourne movie franchise. On multiple occasions, the two friends have worked together and each time, you can expect them to bring the magic.

    4 Kevin Hart And Dwayne Johnson

    Kevin Hart's friendship with WWE star turned actor Dwayne Johnson is not your everyday kind of friendship. But that's what we love about these two; very different yet so alike in a lot of ways. The duo's bromance started while filming the 2016 comedy action flick, Central Intelligence and from there, they have been literally inseparable. Since releasing Central Intelligence, Dwayne and Kevin have worked on many more movies, including the 2017 blockbuster Jumanji. But while we love this duo's on-screen chemistry, what we can't get enough of is their endless banter both in movies and real life.

    3 Jason Momoa And Henry Cavil

    Justice League co-stars Jason Momoa and Henry Cavill ride really hard for each other. From Jason defending each other publicly and giving bear hugs when they can, Jason and Henry have warmed our hearts with their incredible friendship. Despite their seemingly busy schedules, it is not uncommon to find these two hanging out and making memories. Permit us to say we want what these two have!

    Related: 18 Unconventional Celebrity Friendships We Can't Ignore

    2 Vin Diesel And Paul Walker

    Paul Walker passed away in a tragic car crash in 2013 but his friendship with Vin Diesel is one we will always remember. The pair struck a friendship after they were cast for the original Fast and Furious movie in 2001 and in the years that followed, they gave us a glimpse of what true bromance looks like. Following Walker's untimely death, Diesel has continued to find ways to honor his friend. In 2017, the actor announced that he named his newborn daughter Pauline, admitting that it was a way to keep Walker's memory alive. Even better, he has remained very close to Walker's daughter Meadow whom he now considers family.

    1 Mel Brooks And Carl Reiner

    The year was 1950. Carl Reiner met Mel Brooks and struck what would become one of Hollywood's longest-lasting friendships. Seventy years together and Mel and Carl have given us the blueprint of true platonic love. Up until Carl's recent death at the age of 98, the pair often spent their evenings together, eating dinner and watching their favorite shows. We have seen many beautiful friendships but we've quite not seen anything like Mel and Carl's. And we and many others love every bit of it!
