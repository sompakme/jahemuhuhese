    Roger Federer is regarded as one of the great tennis players of all time. With 20 major Grand Slam titles under his belt, Federer has become an influential figure in the world of sports. Beyond the game, Federer, with his charming looks and humble personality, is a popular choice for brand endorsements. Between 2020 and 2021, the athlete earned a whopping $90.6 million from his endorsement deals. While being the face of top brands such as Rolex, Lindt, Barilla, NetJets, and Uniqlo, he is also the brand ambassador for Mercedes-Benz. A brand as elegant as him, Mercedes-Benz has partnered with Federer since 2008. In 2017, the automaker renewed the contract until 2027 and will pay him $5 million every year. Federer will also be rewarded a new Mercedes every six years.

    His expensive contract and love for luxury cars have led Federer to own a couple of Mercedes-Benz classics and a Range Rover in his collection. An extremely tasteful lineup, let’s take a look at Roger Federer’s multi-million dollar car collection.

    Mercedes AMG GT S

    The Mercedes AMG GT S was an ambitious car launched by the automaker in direct competition to Porsche. The car is equipped with a 4.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 that has an output of 503 HP. The car runs from 0 to 60 MPH in 3.5 seconds and comes with a top speed of 193 MPH. With a stylish look and a great sound, it is the most standout vehicle in Roger Federer’s collection. As mentioned by Hot Cars, the AMG GT S has a starting price of $130,000.

    Mercedes AMG GLE G63 Coupe

    Along with owning the Mercedes GLE G663 Coupe, Federer was also appointed to promote the car when it was first released in 2016. A combination of a sporty coupe and robust SUV, the AMG GLE G63 Coupe is an ideal vehicle for people who stay at the top of their game. The car is equipped with a then-brand-new 9G-TRONIC nine-speed automated transmission with a standard 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system, as noted by Mercedes Blog. The 5.5-liter V8 engine cranks out 557 HP and 516 lb-ft of torque. The interior boasts Napa leather seats and a flat-bottomed steering wheel. The base price of the Mercedes AMG GLE G63 Coupe is $109,300.

    Mercedes SL AMG

    A convertible in the collection, Roger Federer owns one of the best cars produced by Mercedes. The convertible is equipped with a supercharged V8 M113 engine with a top speed of 202 MPH. The luxurious interior has a keyless function, an in-built massager in the seats, and heated and ventilated seats. The interior was also upgraded with an Alcantara steering wheel. The cost of the SL AMG is upwards of $91,000.

    Mercedes SLS AMG Roadster

    Roger Federer was one of the first to test-drive the 2012 Mercedes SL AMG Roadster. For the 2012 model, Mercedes abandoned the gullwing doors and opted for the conventional regular-fitted ones. The car is elegantly designed with short-deck proportions and a long hood. The car uses the standard AMG engine, the 6.2-liter V8, that channels 563 HP via the seven-speed dual-clutch automatic gearbox. According to Car And Driver, the car prances from 0 to 60 in 3.5 seconds and has a top speed of 197 MPH. The 2012 Mercedes SLS AMG Roadster costs upwards of $198,675.

    Mercedes CLS 450

    In 2018, Roger Federer won the 40th Mercedes Cup Stuttgart Tennis tournament. Along with the price money, the tennis star also won a Mercedes CLS 450. The CLS 450 is equipped with a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharge inline-six engine that works well with the 48-volt mild hybrid system. The smooth and powerful motor helps the car reach 0 to 62 MPH in merely 4.8 seconds. The luxurious interior offers high-tech and comfortable styling that can be customized based on preference. The regular model of the CLS 450 has a base price of 93,200, while the hybrid model begins at $92,700. The custom model can go as high as $117,810, as stated by Cars Guide.

    Range Rover SVR

    One of the only cars owned by Federer outside the Mercedes lineup is the Range Rover SVR. Equipped with a 5.0-liter V8, the car can hit 62 MPH in 4.5 seconds. The car is designed using Range Rover Sport’s lightweight all-aluminum architecture that offers a robust exterior and opulent interior for a drive. The interior features Windsor leather seats finished in satin black with the SVR logo stitched into the headrests. The car is designed for niche performance-focused clients the enjoy performance and luxury. The starting price of the Range Rover SVR is $168,000.

    Due to his lucrative contract and good relations with Mercedes-Benz, Roger Federer has had the privilege of owning some of the best cars produced by the German automaker. His exclusive deal allows him to get his hands on the best cars even before they have hit the market. With an agreement to acquire a new Mercedes every six months, Roger Federer will have a whole garage in the coming years.
