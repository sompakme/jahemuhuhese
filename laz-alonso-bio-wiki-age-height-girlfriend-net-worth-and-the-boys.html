    Laz Alonso Biography Biography

    Laz “Lazaro” Alonso is an American film and television actor. He is best known for playing Tsu’tey in James Cameron’s science fiction film Avatar,  Fenix Calderon in the film Fast & Furious and Mothers Milk in the superhero series The Boys released in 2019

    He has also appeared in other films such as Jarhead, This Christmas, Miracle at St. Anna, and Detroit. In 2019, he joined the main cast of Amazon Prime Video’s original series The Boys based on the comic book series of the same name. He plays the role of “MM” (Mother’s Milk).

    Laz Alonso Acting Career

    Laz began his entertainment career hosting shows on the BET network. He appeared in music videos by musicians like Toni Braxton and Aaliyah. He later started appearing in films such as This Christmas, Down for Life, Jarhead, G, Leprechaun: Back 2 Tha Hood, Stomp the Yard, and Miracle at St. Anna.

    In 2011, he played as one of the lead characters on the TV show Breakout Kings during its first season and the first episode of season two. He has also made various television guest appearances on Eyes, Bones, CSI: Miami, NCIS, The Practice, and The Unit. In 2013, he was the lead in the NBC drama Deception. Alonso played the role of Detective Billy Soto, on the NBC series The Mysteries of Laura.
