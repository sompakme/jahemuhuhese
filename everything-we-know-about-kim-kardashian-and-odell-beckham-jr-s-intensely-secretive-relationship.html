    Since Kim Kardashian and the Kardashian/Jenner family are so rich, it makes sense that Kim has dated a series of well-off celebrities. Kim stayed wealthy after her divorce from Kanye West and moved on quickly. But while fans learned about Kim and Pete Davidson's relationship, she was much quieter about dating NFL player Odell Beckham Jr.

    Near the end of March 2024, The Daily Mail reported that Odell Beckham Jr. and Kim Kardashian broke up. Their split is just as intriguing as the rest of their romance. In this article, we'll take a look at what we know about this secretive relationship, along with who the reality star and athlete have dated in the past.

    Inside Kim Kardashian And Odell Beckham Jr.'s Private Relationship

    While Kim Kardashian and Odell Beckham Jr. never confirmed their relationship and have never talked about being together, they have sometimes been spotted at public events. Considering that they wanted privacy, this was an interesting choice.

    According to Harper's Bazaar, when looking at the timeline of Beckham Jr. and Kim's relationship, they attended parties for the Super Bowl and Oscars in February and March 2024. It's possible that they didn't care if people saw them together, but they didn't choose to discuss each other in interviews.

    Besides these events, though, the couple wasn't photographed together as much as Kim was with her ex-boyfriends and ex-husbands.

    Fans should know that Odell Beckham Jr. and Kim Kardashian were "casual," at least at the beginning of their romance. A source explained to Us Weekly that the Kardashian/Jenner family was fine with it and that they had "great chemistry." The source also said:

    “There’s nothing official so they’re both allowed to date other people. Although they’re not committed, Kim usually dates one guy at a time, so she’s not seeing anybody else.”

    It sounds like things changed in February 2024. A source told Life & Style Magazine that the couple had gotten more "serious." They said:

    “Kim and Odell have moved from casual dating to being exclusive. It’s serious. Kim and Odell wanted to make sure this wasn’t just a fling. Their connection is real. Kim hasn’t been this happy in a very long time.”

    While neither star has commented on the breakup, a source told Life & Style Magazine that Kim froze her eggs and was interested in having a baby with Beckham Jr.

    It's interesting to see that in contrast to her marriage to Kanye West, Kim didn't want to tell the public that she was dating Beckham Jr., and she didn't discuss him in interviews. This could be because she felt that she rushed into dating after she and West divorced.

    According to Today.com, she said in an episode of The Kardashians:

    “I definitely jumped into another relationship so fast. It got my mind away from stuff and that’s not a way to, like, run from things. It’s better to deal, heal ... then feel.”

    Although fans might be surprised that Kim Kardashian didn't talk about Odell Beckham Jr. since she has been open about some other relationships, the football star has said he is a private person. In 2019, Beckham Jr. talked about his career and love life in an interview with GQ and said that people questioned his sexuality because they didn't know anything about his dating life.

    He said:

    “I just don’t want you to see what woman I’m with, and I don’t want you in my personal life. I always try to keep my personal life my personal life. I feel like I don’t owe that to anybody.”

    Over the years, fans noticed a pattern in Kim Kardashian's dating life: she has been in relationships with many athletes. According to Us Weekly, she said in a 2012 episode of Keeping Up With The Kardashians, "I'm done dating athletes I think." However, while she did marry a rapper after that and then date an actor, her romance with Odell Beckham Jr. proves that she doesn't seem to stick to any rules when it comes to finding love.

    Who Has Odell Beckham Jr. Dated?

    According to Page Six, Odell Beckham Jr. has had one long relationship with Lauren Wood, who is the mother of his child Zydn Beckham. The couple dated from 2019 to 2023.

    As Page Six reported, there have been many dating rumors about Beckham Jr. and other women, but these relationships weren't confirmed. People think that he and Polyxeni Ferfeli, a model, dated from 2017 to 2018. Before that, he and Amber Rose appeared to begin dating in 2015 and broke up in 2017. While fans wondered if he was dating Zendaya and Khloe Kardashian at different points in 2016, the stars said they didn't date.
