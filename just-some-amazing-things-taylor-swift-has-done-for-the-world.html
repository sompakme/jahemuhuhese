    16 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Taylor Swift is one of the world’s best-selling artists. If we were to name some of her accolades, it would take a few months and some change. Swift has worked hard through the years and holds the record as the artist with the most American Music Awards (32), in addition to 11 Grammys, 25 Billboard Music Awards, and over 200 million records sold.

    RELATED: Just Some Amazing Things Justin Bieber Has Done For The World

    More impressive than Swift’s great achievements as an artist, is her heart, which is full of kindness. Over time, Swift has made numerous personal donations to different charities, recorded songs to support worthy causes, pledged to support the building of community projects, and lent her voice to different movements. These are only but a few ways in which Swift has helped make the world a better place:

    10 Supporting ‘Hope For Haiti’

    Following the 2010 Haiti earthquake, which was estimated to have affected at least three million people, various artists, including Alicia Keys, Coldplay, John Legend, Justin Timberlake, Rihanna, and Jay-Z, to name but a few, joined forces to produce a live album. Taylor Swift was also part of the initiative and performed the song ‘Breathless’, written by Kevin Griffin. Sales from the album were all channeled towards relief efforts by the Red Cross and Yele Haiti Foundation.

    9 Donating To Victims Of The Iowa Flood

    The Iowa Flood of 2008 gave birth to the phrase ‘Iowa’s Katrina.’ The floods emerged from rivers in Eastern Iowa and resulted in at least $6 billion worth of damage. Most of those affected were forced to evacuate from their homes. Taylor Swift contributed to relief efforts by donating a sum of $100,000 to add to relief efforts by the Red Cross.

    RELATED: Just Some Amazing Things Beyonce Has Done For The World

    8 Making Contributions Towards Education

    Taylor Swift has made several donations to support education. Back in 2010, she provided $75,000 to aid in the renovation of Hendersonville High School in Nashville. Swift also partnered with Chegg to make donations to music departments of selected colleges. She has donated books to various libraries such as the Nashville Public Library, and in 2012, pledged a total of $4 million to the building of an Education Centre in Nashville.

    7 Participating In Telethons

    In response to the Tennessee floods that occurred in 2010, Swift donated a sum of $500,000 at a telethon hosted by WSMV-TV.Swift also participated in the 'Stand Up to Cancer' telethon where she performed the song ‘Ronan.’ Swift had written the song in memory of a little boy who succumbed to cancer. Proceeds from the song went towards cancer charities.

    6 Making A Wish Come True

    The Make- A- Wish Foundation grants the wishes of terminally ill patients and Taylor Swift has been a participant in the past. Laney Brown, an eight-year-old who was battling Leukemia, had a wish of talking to Taylor Swift. On her birthday, Swift surprised Laney by calling her on Facetime. She was smiling the whole time, even though she was a little nervous. Unfortunately, Laney passed away five days after Taylor’s call, with her final wish having been granted.

    5 Donating Items For Charity Auctions

    Swift has worked with a number of foundations to donate items for charity auctions, including the Elton John AIDS Foundation and Feeding America. In 2020, Swift donated her autographed folklore-themed guitar for WhyHunger, whose bidding was aimed at raising funds for ending world hunger and helping those affected by the pandemic. Similarly, back in 2010, Swift's guitar fetched $16,250 at a charity auction for the Country Music Hall of Fame.

    4 Support For Kesha's Legal Battle

    Not only does Taylor Swift support the general public, but her kind heart also extends towards fellow artists as well. Since 2014, Kesha and music producer Dr. Luke have been involved in a fierce legal battle characterized by lawsuits and countersuits. Kesha accuses the producer of emotional abuse, gender-related discrimination, and breach of contract. Dr. Luke accuses the singer of defamation, and Taylor Swift has made donations in support of Kesha.

    3 Donating To Covid-19 Relief Efforts

    Since March of 2020, the pandemic has completely altered the way we live our lives. Many have lost employment, while others have found new ways to make a living. Celebrities have lent their voices in making sure the message is home; social distancing, getting vaccinated and protecting the ones we love. Taylor Swift contributed to Covid-19 relief efforts by performing at Lady Gaga’s benefit concert. Proceeds from the concert went towards the World Health Organization.

    2 Donating To The 'Black Lives Matter' Movement

    The Black Lives Matter movement is aimed at addressing police brutality and discrimination against people of color. At the very height of the movement, George Floyd was murdered in broad daylight by officer Derek Chauvin in Minneapolis, Minnesota. Swift made donations to the NAACP Legal Defense and Educational Fund, in support of Black Lives Matter.

    1 Standing Up For Women

    Many times in public, Swift has lent her voice to criticizing sexism and misogyny. At the inception of the Times Up Movement, a cause aimed at addressing harassment, Swift was one of the original signatories. She’s also made donations to the Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network as part of Sexual Assault Awareness Month. Swift has publicly encouraged women to engage in politics and celebrate their achievements.
