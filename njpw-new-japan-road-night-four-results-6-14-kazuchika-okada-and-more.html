    NJPW New Japan Road Night Four Results (6/14): Kazuchika Okada And More

    On June 14, New Japan Pro Wrestling continued their New Japan Road series with the fourth night of action from Kuki City Gymnasium in Kuki, Saitama, Japan. In the main event, Los Ingobernables de Japon battled Just 5 Guys. Plus, Kazuchika Okada and other stars competed.

    06 Aug 2024, 00:00

    On June 14, New Japan Pro Wrestling continued their New Japan Road series with the fourth night of action from Kuki City Gymnasium in Kuki, Saitama, Japan.

    In the main event, Los Ingobernables de Japon battled Just 5 Guys. Plus, Kazuchika Okada and other stars competed.

    The results, courtesy of Cagematch, are as follows:

    • Kosei Fujita vs. Ryohei Oiwa ended in a 10-minute time-limit draw.
    • Great Bash Heel (Togi Makabe & Tomoaki Honma) def. Oskar Leube & Oleg Boltin
    • CHAOS (Toru Yano, Hirooki Goto & YOH) & Master Wato def. House Of Torture (SHO, EVIL, Dick Togo & Yujiro Takahashi)
    • United Empire (Great-O-Khan, TJP & Francesco Akira) def. BULLET CLUB War Dogs (Drilla Moloney, Clark Connors & Gedo)
    • BULLET CLUB War Dogs (Gabe Kidd & Alex Coughlin) def. YOSHI-HASHI & Yuto Nakashima
    • Strong Style (Ren Narita & El Desperado), Shota Umino & Tiger Mask def. CHAOS (Kazuchika Okada & Tomohiro Ishii), Hiroshi Tanahashi & Ryusuke Taguchi
    • Los Ingobernables de Japon (Shingo Takagi, Tetsuya Naito, Yota Tsuji, BUSHI & Hiromu Takahashi) def. Just 5 Guys (SANADA, Taichi, TAKA Michinoku, DOUKI & Yoshinobu Kanemaru)
