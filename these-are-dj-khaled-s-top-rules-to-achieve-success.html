    DJ Khaled, born Khaled Mohamed Khaled, is a renowned DJ, record producer, and media personality. With a career spanning over two decades, he has become famous in the music industry, working with some of the most prominent artists and producing chart-topping hits. DJ Khaled's success is a result of his talent, unwavering dedication and adherence to a set of rules that have guided him throughout his journey.

    Apart from being a music mogul, DJ Khaled is also a modern-day philosopher sharing his bit of success mantras regularly through social media.

    Stay Away From ‘They’

    DJ Khaled advises his fans to distance themselves from negative influences, referring to them as ‘they’ as revealed by Miami New Times. He often shows that by surrounding yourself with positive, supportive people, one can maintain a healthy mindset and focus on their goals. He has built a close-knit team of collaborators, including artists, producers, and industry professionals, who have helped him achieve his goals. By surrounding himself with the right people, DJ Khaled has created a network of like-minded individuals who contribute to his success.

    Celebrate Your Success

    As revealed in an interview with NBC News, DJ Khaled believes in glorifying your success. He learned early on in his career that he couldn't wait for someone to give him a pat on the back or to say he had accomplished something great. He had to let them and the world know that he was great. DJ Khaled believes that one can't expect people to hype them up every time.

    Don't Ever Play Yourself

    DJ Khaled believes that complaining is playing yourself. It's a setback at anything you're trying to accomplish. He encourages people to use their energy to create solutions instead of excuses. He also believes that sometimes you must play yourself as a lesson to avoid repeating it, thus becoming more knowledgeable and aware.

    Respect The Code

    Respecting the code is a significant rule in DJ Khaled's life, especially regarding friendship and relationships. Trust is the hardest thing because when someone violates your trust, it can be challenging to return and have those same feelings toward that person. DJ Khaled believes that you have to have a code in your life that represents you. For instance, he permanently disclaims that whatever he says is off the record when he talks to his friends. He wants his friends to talk about their public conversation with his permission.

    Embrace Hard Work And Dedication

    Success doesn't come easy, and DJ Khaled knows this all too well. He has consistently demonstrated a strong work ethic and dedication to his craft, often working long hours in the studio to perfect his music. This commitment to hard work has allowed him to produce hit after hit, solidifying his status as a top-tier music producer.

    A Good Breakfast To Start The Day

    As reported by Acclaim, DJ Khaled Khaled asserts that a vital element in achieving success is having breakfast, especially egg whites. However, Khaled is fortunate to have a personal chef who can prepare a wholesome and delicious breakfast whenever he desires. On the other hand, Khaled’s breakfast usually consists of a simple and quick option like toast with vegemite, provided there is enough time.

    Daily Use Of Cocoa Butter

    Khaled prioritizes keeping his skin healthy and moisturized, and he achieves this by generously applying cocoa butter daily. He ensures that no area is left untreated, even the hard-to-reach spots, and he does this with or without assistance. Cocoa butter helps to moisturize and nourish the skin, prevent dryness, and even reduce the appearance of stretch marks. Such is Khaled’s love for Cocoa butter that he even collaborated with Palmer's Cocoa Butter to release a three-product range of Khaled-approved cocoa butter lotions as per Storefront.

    Never To Ride A Jet-Ski At Night

    As funny and bizarre as it sounds, this is one of DJ Khaled’s success mantras inspired by his real-life experience. In 2015, Khaled decided to go for a nighttime jet ski ride after eating lunch at Rick Ross' house. Unfortunately, he got lost and ended up stranded in the dark for a long time. He reached home hours later and also captured the whole moment on Snapchat. That day he learned a valuable lesson about Never To Ride A Jet-Ski At Night.

    DJ Khaled's journey to success is a flagbearer of the power of hard work, dedication, and following a set of guiding principles. By adhering to these eight rules, as he shared on social media and in interviews, he has achieved incredible success in the music industry and beyond. These rules serve as valuable lessons for anyone looking to achieve their dreams and aspirations, reminding us that anything is possible with the right mindset and approach.
