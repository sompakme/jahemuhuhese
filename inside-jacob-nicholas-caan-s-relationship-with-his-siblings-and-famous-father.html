He began acting in 1961 and ever since then he has been featured in many hit movies, gotten many awards, and left a legacy behind for his children to follow in his footsteps.Jacob Nicholas Caan is the last-born son of all the 5 children the actor had in his 4 marriages, and here is everything we know about his life and relationship with his siblings so far...

    How Old Is Jacob Nicholas Caan?

    According to Distractify, James's first marriage was with Dee Jay Mathis in 1961, but the pair decided to go their separate ways in 1966.

    In this marriage, Jacob's oldest sibling was conceived, and together they had a daughter on November 5, 1964, and named her Tara Caan. She's now 58 years old.

    A few years passed when James decided to marry again for the second time. He fell in love with the actress Sheila Marie Ryan in 1976.

    She was one of Elvis's Presley's ex-girlfriend and a Playboy cover model. Unfortunately, the marriage did not work out, and they decided to call it quits mutually just after one year of being together in 1977.

    James Married again in 1990 but split up with his third wife in 1944. On his 3rd Marriage, James and Ingrid Hajek welcomed a son born on April 10, 1991. His name was Alexander James Caan, and he is currently 31 years old.

    In 1995, On James's last marriage, he got married to Linda Stokes his 4th wife. Their marriage lasted for over 20 years until they divorced in 2016.

    While married to Linda, the couple had 2 sons, James Arthur Caan who was born on November 6, 1995, making him 27 years now, and Jacob Nicholas Caan, born on September 24th, 1998 making him currently 24 years old.

    Jacob was born in Los Angeles California and attended a Roman Catholic Preparatory High School for Boys named La Salle High School based in Cincinnati. Later on, Jacob enrolled at Chapman University and successfully graduated in 2021.
