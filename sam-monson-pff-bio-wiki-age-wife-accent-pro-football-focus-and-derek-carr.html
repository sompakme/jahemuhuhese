    Sam Monson Biography

    Sam Monson is an Irish-American Sports Analyst and Personality who was born and brought-up in the United States. Currently, he works at Pro Football Focus (PFF) in Cincinnati, Ohio as the Lead NFL Analyst. He previously worked for PFF as a Director of Consumer Ops for an year, Lead NFL Analyst for 3 years and initially as a Senior NFL Analyst.

    Sam joined the Pro Football Focus team in 2008, working as a Player Participation Analyst. He began his career working as a Part-Time kids Rugby Coach at Willow Park School in Blackrock, Dublin, Ireland. As one of the initial core team at PFF, and part of the company’s development, early growth, and expansion, he is greatly involved in the data collection process and practice, as well as media and consumer-facing side of the company.
