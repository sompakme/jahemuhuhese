    Emily Blunt became one of the most talked-about attendees at the February 18th BAFTAs, in part due to a rare, expensive Tiffany & Co. necklace Blunt wore on the red carpet.

    During and after The British Academy of Film And Television Arts (BAFTA)'s annual award show, Blunt's ensemble and appearance made headlines.

    According to People magazine, Blunt, 40, was escorted by her parents to the BAFTAs, instead of by husband John Krasinski.

    People described her "beaded" look as "dreamy," reporting:

    "Emily arrived with her parents, Joanna and Oliver Blunt, instead of bringing along her husband, John Krasinski. Blunt, 40, stepped out in a dreamy sheer and metallic Elie Saab Couture gown with cutout details and beading all over. The dress featured dramatic bell sleeves and a train, and she accessorized with a Tiffany & Co. necklace."

    Emily Blunt's Rare Tiffany Necklace

    As stars walked the red carpet for the 2024 BAFTAs, Emily Blunt's eye-catching necklace was a popular topic on social media.

    On February 18th, Tiffany & Co. tweeted about their pieces on the red carpet, including Blunt's stunning necklace:

    Tiffany & Co. identified the glittering piece as the "Jean Schlumberger by Tiffany Fleurage necklace with multicolored gemstones of over 37 total carats."

    On Tiffany.com, the iconic Manhattan jewelry magnate's "High Jewelry" section detailed the brand's most exclusive pieces, with Jean Schlumberger's designs among the rare few.

    According to Tiffany, Schlumberger's designs were pivotal in terms of influence:

    "Jean Schlumberger ushered in an unprecedented era of jewelry design when he began his legendary collaboration with Tiffany & Co. in 1956. With his unique ability to balance jubilant opulence with tasteful restraint, Jean Schlumberger created transcendent jewels — wearable art."

    Tiffany created a landing page for Jean Schlumberger's designs, explaining that "he was known for modernizing impressive, stately jewels in whimsical, dreamlike settings often inspired by nature."

    However, neither Tiffany's Fleurage necklace nor the line to which it belonged appeared on the page.

    A similar – but not identical – Tiffany necklace designed by Schlumberger appeared on an international version of the brand's website (as did a bracelet from the line); no price was listed.

    However, Google search results priced the bracelet at over half a million dollars, $525,000, to be exact.

    How Much Does Emily Blunt's Tiffany & Co. Necklace Cost?

    While prices for other pieces in the Schlumberger Fleurage line were visible in Google search results, we were initially unable to locate any pricing information about the necklace.

    In 2022, Beyoncé wore a Tiffany necklace described as "priceless," and People noted that the piece was not available for purchase.

    However, we located an April 2008 British Vogue article about a very similar Tiffany Fleurage necklace designed by Jean Schlumberger, with clear diamonds instead of colored diamonds.

    According to Vogue UK, the Tiffany Fleurage necklace featured was priced at £227,500 in 2008, or $286,415 in today's dollars:

    "His Signature of Chic collection - with the magnificent Fleurage necklace (£227,500) as its centrepiece and the Daisy bracelet a more than acceptable accompaniment - features enamel, 18-carat gold and diamonds."

    Tiffany & Co.'s "high jewelry" rarely if ever features a price tag on their website – very much in an "if you have to ask, you can't afford it" fashion – and the exact value of the necklace remains undisclosed.

    Given that Emily Blunt's Tiffany Fleurage necklace is not listed for sale and likely boasts rarer diamonds, it is almost certainly worth more than $300,000.
