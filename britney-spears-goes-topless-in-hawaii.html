    The best shape of her life. Britney Spears showed off her enviably slim figure over the weekend as she soaked up the sun during a vacation in Hawaii.

    The “Private Show” singer, 34, shared a sexy photo of herself posing topless on the deck of a boat, looking out over crystal clear blue Hawaiian waters, via Instagram Saturday, August 6.

    “Still dreaming a mile a minute,” she captioned the colorful shot using a lyric from her recent single “Make Me.”

    In the shot, Spears showcases her incredibly toned back and pert derrière in a tiny white bikini bottom with her back facing the camera. As she looks to the side in a pair of white sunglasses, she seemingly covers her breasts with the top half of the swimsuit.

    Earlier in the week, the pop princess relaxed on the island of Kauai in a white halter bikini, red shades and a brown fedora while her two sons, Sean Preston, 10, and Jayden James, 9, played nearby in the ocean.

    As fans know, Spears is no stranger to flaunting her svelte body on social media. Earlier this summer, she showed off her trim abs in a neon peach bikini, just days after she danced to her 2003 tune “Breathe on Me” in a series of seductive Instagram videos.

    The “Toxic” songstress will release her ninth studio album, Glory, on Friday, August 26.
