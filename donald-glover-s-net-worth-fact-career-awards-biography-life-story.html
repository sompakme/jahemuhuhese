    Early Life of Childish Gambino

    Childish Gambino was born in Edwards Air Force Base, California as Donald McKinley Glover Jr. He is American by nationality and belongs to black ethnicity. His zodiac sign is Libra. 

    His real name is Donald Glover. He performs music under the stage name Childish Gambino and as a DJ under the name mcDJ. 

    He was born to parents, Beverly and Donald Glover Sr. His father is a retired postal worker where his mother is a retired daycare provider. He has a brother Stephen and a sister Bree. 

    Moving towards his education, He attended DeKalb Schoo of the Arts. Later, He graduated from New York University Tisch School of Arts with a degree in Dramatic Writing in 2006.

    Career of Childish Gambino

    Childish Gambino kick-started his career as a writer for NBC’s sitcom 30 rock. He was the writer for three seasons in 30 rock where he also got an award for Best Comedy Series in Writers Guild for America Award in February 2009. He also played the role of Ex-President Barack Obama on Saturday Night Live. He is also a member of Derrick Comedy and wrote a film named Mystery film which released in theatres in 2009.

    Later, in 2008, he also began his career as a singer with independent mixtape ‘Sick Boi.’ After that, He released a series of mixtapes like “I am just a rapper” and “I am just a rapper2” followed by his 5th mixtape Culdasac on July 2010. On 15th November 2011, He released his album Camp which had singles like Bonfire and Heartbeat which ranked 18 under hot 100 singles and 54th on hot R&B songs. After that, he released his second album, “Because the internet” which hit no seven on the Billboard 200 chart.

    Furthermore, in 2015, He appeared in three movies, The Martian, Magic Like XXL and The Lazarus Effect. However, his role in Spiderman Homecoming (2017) is the one which helped him to enhance his career. The buzz is, he will be a part of the Star Wars movie (2018). Currently, He is focused on playing his role as Simba in the upcoming The Lion King. He is also busy in filing the movie Guava Island. The movie is currently in its post-production phase.

    Awards and Achievements of Childish Gambino

    In his career so far, He has been nominated for a total of one hundred thirty awards, out of which he has won thirty-six. Some of the awards he has won include ‘The Comedy Award’ in 2012 for Breakout Performance of the Year for his role in the TV series ‘Community’, and two Golden Globe Awards in 2017 for Best Television series and Best Actor for the TV series ‘Atlanta.’
