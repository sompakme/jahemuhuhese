    Mercy Okojie Johnson is a Nigerian actress, producer, and businesswoman. Mercy Johnson was born on 28 August 184 at Lagos, Lagos State, Nigeria to Daniel Johnson, former naval officer and Elizabeth Johnson who are all Nigerians.

    Mercy Johnson started her education in Calabar and later went to Lagos state to continue her primary education a Navy Primary School. Mercy Johnson attended Rivers state secondary school and later went to Nigerian Navy Secondary School in Port Harcourt, Rivers State. Mercy Johnson attended several schools because her father was transferred to different stations to work and the entire family must move to where her father is being transferred to.

    Mercy Johnson started her career as a professional actress shortly after she graduated from the secondary school. She was given a roe to play in the movie called “The Maid”. This was the beginning of the success story of Mercy Johnson. She has then subsequently acted in other movies such as Hustlers, Baby Oku in America, War in the Palace, and many other top hit movies. In 2013, Mercy Johnson won the 2013 Africa Magic viewers choice awards for her role in the comedy movie “Dumebi The Dirty Girl.”
