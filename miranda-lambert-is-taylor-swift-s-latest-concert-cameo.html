    Miranda Lambert Is Taylor Swift's Latest Concert Cameo

    Taylor Swift goes country again! On Wednesday, Oct. 21, Miranda Lambert joined Swift onstage at her concert in Greensboro, N.C., as the two belted Lamberts hit Little Red Wagon from her smash 2014 album Platinum.

    17 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Taylor Swift goes country again! On Wednesday, Oct. 21, Miranda Lambert joined Swift onstage at her concert in Greensboro, N.C., as the two belted Lambert’s hit “Little Red Wagon” from her smash 2014 album Platinum.

    The superstar, 25, shared two clips and a photo on Instagram of the amazing duet. The first showed Lambert, 31, singing her song, with Tay joining her halfway through. “YOU CAN’T RIDE IN MY LITTLE RED WAGON,” Swift captioned.

    In the second clip, Blake Shelton’s ex-wife and the “Wildest Dreams” singer are shown rocking out front and center to the 2012 country smash. “Oooooooh, aaaaah,” Tay captioned in a nod to the signature catchy chorus.

    And in a black-and-white photo that Calvin Harris’ girlfriend shared of the two singers looking fierce onstage, Swift captioned, “And you’re just trying to slow this rolling stone but I’m onto you babe… @mirandalambert.”
