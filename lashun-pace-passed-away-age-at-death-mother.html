    LaShun Pace, a member of the famed trio The Anointed Pace Sisters, died at the age of 60, sending shockwaves through Gospel music.

    According to a number of sources, including Dorinda Clark-Cole and Karen Clark Sheard, news about Pace, best known for her signature song ‘I Know I’ve Been Changed,’ ‘There’s a Leak in This Old Building,’ and the Karen Clark Sheard-assisted 1996 hit ‘Act Like You Know’ (which recently went viral thanks to a TikTok trend), began surfacing on the internet on Monday (March 21).

    The exact cause of death for the Christian Music Hall of Famer has yet to be revealed, and the family has yet to issue an official statement.

    She died at the age of 82 on Tuesday, July 7, 2020, in Atlanta, Georgia.
