    Who Is Spencer Rattler?

    Spencer Rattler is an American football quarterback who plays for the University of South Carolina.

    Born on September 28, 2000, Rattler played high school football at Pinnacle High School in Phoenix, Arizona.

    On completion of high school, Rattler had thrown for 11,083 yards in his four years with 116 passing TDs and rushed for 1,040 yards with 14 rushing TDs.

    Also at the time, he won the MVP of the Elite 11.

    Rattler proceeded to play college football at Oklahoma before he was transferred to the University of South Carolina, where he now plays for the South Carolina Gamecocks.

    While at Oklahoma, Rattler was benched during the second quarter in a match against the Texans.

    In 2021, he agreed to a transfer to the University of South Carolina, where he is being coached by Mike Giovando.
